Apex Intelligence is a **color-coded operational framework** for building, tracking, and optimizing affiliate marketing systems for digital services.

- Click-through rate (CTR) (% who clicked affiliate links)
- Conversion rate (% who purchased)
- Revenue per click (RPC)
- Partner performance by ID
- Geographic distribution
- Device breakdown (mobile vs desktop)

### 6. Growth & Visibility Strategy

**Organic First:**
- SEO optimization (keywords, content structure)
- Email list building and segmentation
- Social proof aggregation (testimonials, case studies)
- Content marketing (blogs, videos, guides)

**Paid Scaling (Only After Organic Validation):**
- Facebook/Instagram ads (with proven creatives)
- Google Ads (search + display for high-intent keywords)
- YouTube pre-roll (using top-performing video assets)
- Influencer partnerships (tracked via unique affiliate links)

**Ethical Standards:**
- All services must align with healthy-lifestyle values
- No predatory pricing or misleading claims
- Transparent affiliate disclosures
- Focus on genuine value delivery
